Biriyani

Biriyani is one of the best delicious foods in India. It is a combination of rice, meat and potato with a lovely flavour and fragrance.

  • Rice
  • Meat
  • Vegetables
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Fry the onion and other vegetables.
  2. Boil rice and meat properly.
  3. Add spices according to taste with the vegetables, mainly Biriyani Masala.
  4. In a big bowl, add vegetables, rice and meat in layers.
  5. Add some aroma in the mixture
  6. Keep it under heat for 30 minutes.Make sure the bowl is properly sealed.
  7. Your Biriyani is ready to serve.
