Egg Roll

Egg Roll is one of the best delicious foods in India. It is a combination of egg and flour.

  • Egg
  • Flour
  • Salad
  • Ketchup

Steps

  1. Make a dough with flour.
  2. Give a round and thin shape to the dough and fry on a pan by adding oil.
  3. Make an omlette and fry it with the previous flour product.
  4. Then add some salt to it.
  5. Put it in a plate and add some salad to it.
  6. Fill it with ketchup and roll it.
  7. Your Eggroll is ready to serve.
