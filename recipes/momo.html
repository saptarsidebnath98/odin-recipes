Veg Momo

Momo is one of the best delicious foods in India. It is eaten with soup and spicy tomato ketchup.

  • Vegetables
  • Flour
  • Soup
  • Tomato Ketchup

Steps

  1. Prepare fresh vegetable stuffing.
  2. Bind the dough, fill rolled dough with mixed vegetable mixture.
  3. Give them a “Potli” shape.
  4. Steam cook them.
  5. Your Momo is ready to serve.
